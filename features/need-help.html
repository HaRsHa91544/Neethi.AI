Frequently Asked Questions

How does Neethi.AI check if news is fake or real?

Neethi.AI verifies news in real-time by cross-checking it with 50+ trusted sources like Times of India, NDTV, Eenadu, PIB India, and more. It also uses advanced AI models to analyze credibility, context, and local relevance.

Can Neethi.AI detect local and regional news?

Yes. Neethi.AI specializes in hyperlocal detection, meaning it can verify news from your town, city, or district, and in your local language.

What types of content can Neethi.AI check?

You can verify news articles and headlines, social media posts, website URLs, and images for deepfakes.

Is my personal data safe when I use Neethi.AI?

Absolutely. Neethi.AI only uses the content you submit for verification. We do not sell or share your personal details, and all checks happen securely.

How long does it take to get results?

Most results are provided instantly (within seconds). In rare cases where extra verification is needed, it may take a few minutes.
